KSP Rwanda Scholarship



If you have finished s3 or s6 you are allowed to ask to study on scholarship. People from kayonza district will submit their documents at school of youth
On partnership of KSP-RWANDA and KAYONZA District through school of Kayonza Youthfriendly center, there are communicating the Youth of kayonza district that they are providing studies on free to those who are interested

N.B: Applicants should have finished Ordinary level or Advanceed leve



Study Programmes


  • ➤ FILMMAKING & VIDEO PRODUCTION
  • ➤ HOSPITALITY
  • ➤ MULTIMEDIA
  • ➤ PHOTOGRAPHY & GRAPHIC DESIGN
  • ➤ MUSIC
  • ➤ COMPUTER
  • ➤ JOURNALISM & COMMUNICATION
  • ➤ LANGUEGES: ENGLISH, FRENCH

Requirements:

  • 1. Official letter requesting to study
  • 2. Proof of current level of studies
  • 3. National ID
  • 4. Application fee: 1200 Rwf
