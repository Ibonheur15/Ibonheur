GOVERNMENT OF RWANDA-STUDY LOANS IN HIGHLY SPECIALIZED DIGITAL SKILLS



The Higher Education Council (HEC) and Rwanda Information Society Authority (RISA) wishtoinform the public that through the Rwanda Digital Acceleration Project funded by the WorldBank and Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB), the Government of Rwanda will offer study loans for Rwandans wishing to pursue their higher education in highly specializeddigital skills for Bachelors, Masters, and PhD levels.



Interested candidates should submit an admission letter to one of the following programmes:


  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Robotics
  • Blockchain
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • IoT- Embedded ComputingSystems
  • IoT-Wireless Intelligent Sensors Networking
  • Embedded Computing
  • Wireless Sensor Networking
  • Data Science
  • Cybersecurity
  • Big data
  • Virtual Reality
  • Space technology
  • SoftwareEngineering
  • Computer Science
  • Computer Engineering,
  • Digital Media
  • Digital entrepreneurship

Interested candidates should submit the following application documents:


  • Application letter addressed to the Director General of HEC clearly indicating the level andprogramme applied for, and stating the relevancy of the programme to Rwanda as well ashowthey would use a tech-enabled solution to solve societal problems in Rwanda.
  • Admission letter for candidates stating the name of the course/programme and durationof study.
  • National ID or valid passport
  • Curriculum Vitae
  • Recommendation letters (two letters) these can come from the candidate's head teacher/classteacher, employer(s) and/or academic supervisor.
  • Certified copies of academic documents: certified copies of high school diploma and transcripts of the last two high school years for Bachelor's applicants; and certified degreesandtranscripts of the previous degrees for Masters and PhD applicants.


Please note:


A complete application file should be submitted online via the link https://mis.hec.gov.rw/system-login (New users of the system should first sign up beforeloginand download a user guide for instructions on how to apply).
