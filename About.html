About Us

Where do you want to study?

We are helping students find and fund thier education.

scholorships and Universities are just the begnining of a student's journey


IBomis is a small group which provides educational Information especially University and scholarships to students who finished high School. We help students to know how to apply in different universities both inside and outside of the country. Also will prepare you to be a good applicant who can win scholarships in more than one universities.


Our background/History


IBomis started by Bonheur, after completing high School in Mathematics Economics and Computer science (MCE). After completing his high School he struggled to find all information about universities and scholarships as a student who wishes to continue in University. After that he generated an Aida to building website which will help students to get all information that he lacked after high School.


OUR MISSION


IBomis is an educational, informational website that provides information and news related to the universities operating and. We mainly aim at providing on date information for our readers and other professional help they may need. Our main goal is to help more than 3000 students this year secure places in their favorite universities and pursue their career of choice.
