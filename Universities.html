The Adventist University of Central Africa(AUCA) also called in French: Universite Adventiste d'Afrique Centrale is a Seventh-day Adventist institution of higher learning near two campuses in Kigali, Rwanda. It is a part of the Seventh-day Adventist education system, the world's second largest Christian school system.
